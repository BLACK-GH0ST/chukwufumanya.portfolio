Overview

Chinese Boss is a 2D-in-3D game design focused on clear character readability, level composition, and immersive world-building. The project explores how gameplay, visual style, and performance come together to create a fun and engaging player experience.

Game Design Highlights

Character Expression

2D characters animated with clear expressions to enhance player connection and personality.

Visual Readability

Designed 2D characters and UI elements to stand out against a 3D environment for quick recognition.

Artistic Integration

Blended 2D art style seamlessly into 3D world, maintaining consistency in lighting, scale, and perspective.

Performance Optimized

Balanced sprite and 3D asset complexity to ensure smooth gameplay across devices.

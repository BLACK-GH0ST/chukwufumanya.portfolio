Horror Deception is an atmosphere-driven prototype built around interaction and environmental storytelling. The project showcases how I structure gameplay systems to support pacing, suspense, and player-driven discovery.

Interaction systems

Built interactive objects and triggers to support exploration.

Performance

Kept the experience smooth while maintaining atmosphere-heavy visuals.
